What We Do

Six disciplines. One standard: delivery.

Every engagement runs as a controlled project — defined scope, dependency-mapped schedule, live risk register, and reporting an executive can act on. Technology is our deepest bench, but the discipline applies anywhere work has to ship.

IT Project & Program Management

Full-lifecycle management from initiation through closeout: scope, milestones, budget, RAID risk management, change control, and executive reporting. Waterfall, Agile/Scrum, or hybrid — matched to the work.

Application & Infrastructure Migration

Dependency-mapped cutover planning for application portfolios, server consolidation, and communications systems. Benchmark: 200+ applications moved across six teams, zero production outages.

Release & Deployment Management

Entry/exit criteria, staged rollouts, UAT and QA coordination, defect triage, and post-release monitoring with automated release gates. Every release treated as a controlled project cycle.

Management Consulting & Business Operations

Process redesign, vendor selection, operational tooling, and delivery governance for organizations of any kind — the same discipline we apply to technology, aimed at how your business runs.

Training & Workforce Development

Professional and management training, computer and systems training, and the process documentation that makes it stick — built by someone who has run 100+ person delivery organizations.

Technical Writing & Documentation

SOPs, runbooks, transition plans, and compliance documentation. Clear, complete, and audit-ready — because undocumented delivery is unfinished delivery.

Dawayne Flemming, MBA

Founder · Product & Project Delivery Lead

Dawayne Flemming has spent his career on one problem: getting complex work delivered — completely, on schedule, and without breaking what's already running. Over eight years at DXC Technology he managed the full project lifecycle for enterprise infrastructure programs on two of the most demanding accounts in the industry: UBS, where he ran a global VoIP transformation across 350+ locations in a regulated financial environment, and Lockheed Martin, where he directed the migration of more than 200 business applications with zero production outages.

In 2018 he founded Flemming Consulting Group as an independent product and delivery studio — and proved the discipline transfers. He has since shipped two consumer applications end-to-end to the Apple App Store and Google Play as sole owner of scope, schedule, QA, compliance, and release, while managing technology and operations projects for a portfolio of family-owned small businesses.

He completed his MBA in December 2025, summa cum laude, and returned his practice to its center of gravity: enterprise-grade delivery for organizations that need it done right the first time.
